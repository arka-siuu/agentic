About SAHAYAK

The Mission

SAHAYAK is designed to empower teachers in under-resourced, multi-grade classrooms across India with AI-powered insights and actionable teaching strategies.

Key Features

  • Individual Student Analysis: Detailed AI insights with specific intervention strategies
  • Class-Wide Analytics: Multi-grade classroom management recommendations
  • Zero-Cost Solutions: Practical interventions using available resources
  • Comprehensive Reports: Professional PDF reports with visualizations

Technology

Built with advanced AI language models specifically trained to understand the unique challenges of Indian multi-grade classrooms. The system provides contextually relevant, culturally appropriate recommendations.
